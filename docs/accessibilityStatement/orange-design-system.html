Accessibility statement : Orange Design System

Context

Orange undertakes to make its websites internet, intranet, extranet and its mobile applications accessible in accordance with article 47 of Law No. 2005-102 of February 11, 2005.

To this end, it implements the following strategy and actions :

the pluri-annual accessibility framework (French).

This accessibility statement applies to Orange Design System iOS application.

Conformity level:

"Orange Design System" is partially compliant to the criteria with WCAG version 2.2, level AA.

The Orange Digital Accessibility Expertise Centre refers to the WCAG international recommendations. Here is the correspondence link between the RGAA and the WCAG.

The audit was carried out via la Va11ydette.

Feedback and contact information

If you are unable to access content or a service, you can contact the site manager to be directed to an accessible alternative or obtain the content in another form.

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of this site. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility problems by sending an email to accessibility.group@orange.com.

Legal remedies

You have reported to the website manager an accessibility problem that prevents you from accessing content or one of the portal's services and you have not received a satisfactory response, in this case:

  • Write a message to the Défenseurs des Droits.
  • Or contact the Défenseurs des Droits delegate in your department.
  • Or send a letter by post (free of charge, do not put a stamp)
